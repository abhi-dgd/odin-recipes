Lasagna Recipe

Ingredients

  • Flat pasta sheets
  • Tomato
  • Garlic
  • Rosemary
  • Dry chili flakes
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Chop 5 cloves of garlic.
  2. Heat some oil in a pan. Add chopped garlic.
  3. Throw in some dried chilli flakes.
  4. Add three freshly crushed tomatoes.
  5. Cook sauce for upto 10 minutes.
  6. Add pasta sheets to a baking tray and layer with sauce.
  7. Layer upto 5 times and bake in a 400F oven for 20 minutes.
